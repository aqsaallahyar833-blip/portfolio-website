About Me

I’m a passionate Web Developer with a strong focus on building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I enjoy turning ideas into functional and visually appealing digital experiences.

As a student at the University of Lahore, Sargodha Campus, I have worked on a variety of academic and personal projects that have helped me develop a solid foundation in front-end development. From portfolio websites to interactive web applications, I focus on writing clean, structured code and creating designs that provide a smooth user experience.

I am constantly learning and improving my skills by experimenting with new concepts and refining my previous work. I also use GitHub to manage my projects and track my progress, which helps me stay organized and follow professional development practices.

My goal is to grow as a developer, work on real-world projects, and create websites that not only look good but also perform efficiently. I’m always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and challenges that help me improve and expand my skill set.
